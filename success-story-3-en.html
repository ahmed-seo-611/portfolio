The Challenge — Slow Site in a Highly Competitive Field

Vision Center faced a double challenge: the old website was extremely slow and not mobile-friendly. On top of that, the LASIK eye surgery field is among the most competitive in local search.

Despite the center having modern equipment and outstanding medical staff, the digital presence didn't reflect this level at all. Patients searching for LASIK were finding competitors first.

Keywords like "LASIK eye surgery" and "cataract surgery" are highly competitive and require a strong SEO strategy to rank for them.

2

The Solution — Speed + Medical Content + Specialized SEO

We worked on three parallel axes for faster results:

Complete technical optimization: We rebuilt the site from scratch with a custom design achieving 99+ on PageSpeed. We used WebP for images, Lazy Loading, and optimized Core Web Vitals for the best user experience scores.

E-E-A-T medical content: We created trusted medical content aligned with Google's guidelines for medical sites (E-E-A-T), including detailed pages for each procedure, educational articles, and FAQs for each specialty.

Long-tail keyword strategy: Instead of targeting highly competitive general keywords, we focused first on Long-tail Keywords that are less competitive but have higher purchase intent, delivering faster results.
