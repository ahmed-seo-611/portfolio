The Challenge

Dr. Khalid came to me with an outdated, slow website that didn't appear in search results at all. Despite his long experience in orthopedic surgery and excellent local reputation, new patients were only reaching him through personal referrals.

The core problem was clear: the website didn't reflect the doctor's professional level, its speed was catastrophic, and there was no SEO strategy whatsoever. Competitors dominated the first page while the doctor was completely absent.

The old website took more than 8 seconds to load — causing 70% of visitors to leave before seeing any content.

2

The Solution — An Integrated Strategy

We started with a complete rebuild on WordPress using a custom theme that reflects the doctor's professionalism. We focused on three pillars:

Design: A clean, medical website with trust-inspiring colors, dedicated pages for each surgical service, professional imagery, and an easy-to-use interface for booking and inquiries.

Speed: Comprehensive performance optimization — image compression, caching, CDN, and ensuring a PageSpeed Score of no less than 95 on both mobile and desktop.

Local SEO: Deep keyword research (city name + specialty), trusted medical content creation, Google Business Profile optimization, and building reliable local backlinks.
