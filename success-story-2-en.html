The Challenge — Fierce Local Competition

Gulf AC Company was relying solely on word-of-mouth and paid ads with no effective digital presence. Major competitors dominated Google results while the company didn't even appear on the third page.

The biggest challenge: how to build a strong, sustainable presence in local search without continuously burning budget on paid ads?

The goal: achieve sustainable organic ranking that brings in new customers daily with no long-term additional cost.

2

The Solution — Complete Local SEO

We built a professional WordPress site with full focus on local SEO. Here are the key pillars:

Local keyword research: Comprehensive research on the terms customers in the area use when searching for AC services — "AC maintenance + city name", "AC installation + neighborhood", and similar.

Google Business Profile: Full optimization of the Google Maps listing — professional photos, business hours, services, and regular review responses.

Localized content: Creating articles and blog content targeting customers in specific areas, with local backlinks from trusted regional directories and websites.
